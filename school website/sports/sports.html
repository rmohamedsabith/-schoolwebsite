Sports

Sports play a pivotal role in shaping one’s personality and maintaining good health. We’ve specially developed a sports environment that matches international standards and gives a truly global experience to all our students. All of which is provided to them on the College

Facilities We Offer

There are multiple sport facilities to keep our students engaged and physically fit.

Outdoor and Indoor Sports Facilities

Every student is encouraged to take active part in at least one outdoor activity. SPMS College has set up facilities/grounds for Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Badminton, Chess, etc., and league matches are encouraged. We are also planning an inter-college sports tournaments to develop a spirit of healthy competition.

Special Coaching

We are proud to have quite a few states-level players who have played in the Nationals. One of our students was selected for the heats of a 5000 meter race in the Commonwealth games. Another student has represented India in Volleyball .We plan to arrange special coaching for them so that they make our nation proud.

Gymnasium

In order to ensure a sound body and mind, students are given special attention on health and fitness. A modern gym, with all the latest gadgets has been set up in the hostel.
